# Product Guidelines: HandyHelper

## User Experience (UX) Principles
*   **Accessibility First**: Hands-free interactions via voice commands and real-time audio feedback.
*   **Minimal Cognitive Load**: Present one clear, actionable instruction at a time.
*   **Safety Awareness**: Use non-intrusive audio and UI to ensure users remain aware of their physical surroundings.
*   **Consistency**: Maintain uniform terminology for parts, tools, and actions across all manuals and instructions.

## Visual Design & Aesthetics
*   **High-Contrast UI**: Ensure clear readability in varying lighting conditions (e.g., bright workshops or dimly lit rooms).
*   **Aesthetic Polish**: Modern, minimalist interface with consistent spacing and interaction feedback.
*   **Visual Prompts**: Use simple geometric overlays for object highlighting when POV is available.

## Tone & Voice
*   **Instructional & Encouraging**: The AI assistant should sound helpful and clear, using direct but polite language.
*   **Brevity**: Keep audio instructions concise and focused on the immediate task.

## Interaction Patterns
*   **Confirmations**: Provide immediate auditory feedback for voice commands (e.g., "Moving to step 5" or "Repeating instruction").
*   **Proactive Alerts**: Gently nudge the user if a potential error is detected (e.g., "That looks like the wrong screw").
